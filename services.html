Services

Clear, friendly description of what we do (and do not do).

What We Provide

  • Secure platform for Clients to create and manage home-project posts with files and details.
  • Discovery for subscribed Pros, with masking until a Pro is shortlisted by the Client.
  • Controlled access to project assets using time-limited signed links.
  • Basic messaging/communications between Clients and Pros.
  • Administrative support for account setup and help documentation.

What We Don’t Provide

  • We do not perform construction/repair services ourselves.
  • We do not guarantee prices, timelines, compliance, permits, or outcomes.
  • We do not arbitrate disputes or guarantee payments between Clients and Pros.

Friendly Note

We aim to keep the experience respectful and private: your project details stay private until you choose which Pros can view them. If you have questions, reach us at contact@probuildzone.com.
